# Snack Ideas

Tag legend: `[HP]` high protein · `[LC]` lower carb · `[HC]` higher carb · `[Q]` quick/low-effort

---

### Protein-Forward
- Protein shake + a piece of fruit `[HP]` `[Q]`
- Cottage cheese + berries `[HP]`
- Handful of beef or turkey jerky `[HP]` `[LC]` `[Q]`
- Hard-boiled eggs (2) `[HP]` `[LC]` `[Q]`

### Quick & Easy
- Apple + peanut butter `[Q]`
- Rice cakes + almond butter `[Q]`
- Handful of almonds or walnuts `[LC]` `[Q]`
- Greek yogurt cup `[HP]` `[Q]`

### Higher Volume / Lower Calorie
- Cucumber + hummus `[LC]`
- Air-popped popcorn `[HC]`
- Bell pepper strips + light dressing `[LC]`

### Pre/Post Workout
- Banana + a scoop of protein powder mixed with water `[HP]`
- Rice cakes + honey (pre-workout quick carbs)
- Protein bar (check label — many are closer to a candy bar than a snack)
