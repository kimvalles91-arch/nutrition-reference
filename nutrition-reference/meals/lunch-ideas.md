# Lunch Ideas

Tag legend: `[HP]` high protein · `[LC]` lower carb · `[HC]` higher carb · `[Q]` quick/low-effort · `[DF]` dairy-free option available

---

### Chicken & Rice Bowl `[HP]`
- 6 oz grilled chicken breast
- 1 cup cooked rice
- Roasted broccoli or peppers
- Drizzle of olive oil
**Swap:** ground turkey or salmon for chicken · sweet potato for rice
**Est. macros (base):** ~560 kcal · 53P / 56C / 16F

### Big Salad, Built Right `[LC]` `[Q]`
- Mixed greens base (2 cups)
- 5 oz protein of choice (chicken, shrimp, tofu)
- 1/2 cup quinoa or a handful of tortilla strips
- 1/2 avocado + light dressing
**Swap:** any liked veggie works — this is about volume + protein
**Est. macros (base):** ~480 kcal · 40P / 28C / 22F

### Turkey Wrap `[Q]`
- Large tortilla
- 4 oz sliced turkey
- Cheese, lettuce, tomato, mustard or light mayo
- Side of fruit
**Swap:** lettuce wrap instead of tortilla for lower carb
**Est. macros (base):** ~450 kcal · 35P / 38C / 16F

### Tuna & Crackers `[HP]` `[Q]`
- 1 can tuna in water, mixed with light mayo or Greek yogurt
- Whole grain crackers or a tortilla
- Cucumber/tomato on the side
**Swap:** white beans mixed in for extra fiber + carbs
**Est. macros (base):** ~400 kcal · 36P / 30C / 12F

### Burrito Bowl `[HP]` `[HC]`
- 5 oz ground beef or chicken
- 1 cup rice
- Black beans, salsa, lettuce
- Small amount of cheese
**Swap:** cauliflower rice for lower carb version
**Est. macros (base):** ~620 kcal · 45P / 65C / 18F

### Leftovers Plate `[Q]`
- Whatever protein/carb/veg you cooked the night before, reheated
- This is genuinely a legitimate lunch — cook once, eat twice
**Note:** great default for low-cook-time clients
