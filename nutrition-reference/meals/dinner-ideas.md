# Dinner Ideas

Tag legend: `[HP]` high protein · `[LC]` lower carb · `[HC]` higher carb · `[Q]` quick/low-effort · `[DF]` dairy-free option available

---

### Salmon & Potatoes `[HP]`
- 5-6 oz salmon, baked or pan-seared
- 1 medium potato, roasted
- Asparagus or green beans
**Swap:** white fish or chicken thigh instead of salmon if budget's tight
**Est. macros (base):** ~530 kcal · 40P / 38C / 22F

### Beef & Pasta `[HC]`
- 5 oz lean ground beef
- 1 cup pasta with light sauce
- Side salad or sautéed spinach
**Swap:** turkey for beef · zucchini noodles for lower-carb version
**Est. macros (base):** ~560 kcal · 38P / 50C / 18F

### Sheet Pan Chicken & Veg `[HP]` `[Q]`
- 6 oz chicken thigh or breast
- Mixed vegetables (peppers, zucchini, broccoli) roasted with olive oil
- 1 cup rice or potatoes on the side
**Swap:** swap any veg for what's on the like-list — this is endlessly flexible
**Est. macros (base):** ~520 kcal · 45P / 42C / 18F

### Shrimp Stir-Fry `[LC]`
- 6 oz shrimp
- Mixed vegetables, stir-fried
- 1/2 cup rice
- Light soy or teriyaki sauce
**Swap:** chicken or tofu for shrimp
**Est. macros (base):** ~420 kcal · 38P / 38C / 10F

### Turkey Chili `[HP]` `[HC]`
- Ground turkey
- Kidney/black beans, tomatoes, onions, peppers
- Batch-cookable, great for meal prep
**Swap:** add extra beans for more fiber/carbs without much fat
**Est. macros (base, ~1.5 cups):** ~480 kcal · 40P / 45C / 14F

### Tofu/Chicken Curry `[DF]`
- 5 oz tofu or chicken
- Vegetables + light curry sauce (coconut milk or tomato-based)
- 1 cup rice
**Swap:** coconut milk increases fat — use tomato-based for leaner version
**Est. macros (base):** ~550 kcal · 32P / 55C / 20F
