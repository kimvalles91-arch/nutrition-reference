# Breakfast Ideas

Tag legend: `[HP]` high protein · `[LC]` lower carb · `[HC]` higher carb · `[Q]` quick/low-effort · `[DF]` dairy-free option available

---

### Eggs + Oats Combo `[HP]`
- 2 whole eggs + 1/2 cup egg whites, scrambled
- 1/2 cup oats, cooked with water or milk
- 1/2 cup berries on top
**Swap:** rice instead of oats · turkey bacon for extra protein and volume
**Est. macros (base):** ~430 kcal · 34P / 42C / 14F

### Greek Yogurt Bowl `[HP]` `[Q]`
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp peanut or almond butter
- Handful of granola or 1 banana, sliced
**Swap:** cottage cheese instead of yogurt for thicker texture · `[DF]` use plant yogurt
**Est. macros (base):** ~420 kcal · 27P / 45C / 16F

### Protein Oats `[HP]` `[Q]`
- 1/2 cup oats cooked
- 1 scoop protein powder stirred in once cooked
- Cinnamon + a few berries
**Swap:** swap protein powder flavor, add nut butter for more fat/calories
**Est. macros (base):** ~330 kcal · 29P / 38C / 6F

### Savory Breakfast Bowl `[LC]`
- 2-3 eggs scrambled
- Sautéed spinach + mushrooms
- 1/2 avocado
**Swap:** add black beans for more carbs/fiber if needed
**Est. macros (base):** ~380 kcal · 22P / 12C / 27F

### Cottage Cheese Toast `[HP]` `[Q]`
- 1 cup cottage cheese
- 1-2 slices sourdough, toasted
- Sliced tomato or everything-bagel seasoning on top
**Swap:** whole grain bread instead of sourdough
**Est. macros (base):** ~390 kcal · 35P / 38C / 6F

### Smoothie `[Q]` `[DF possible]`
- 1 scoop protein powder
- 1 cup milk (dairy or plant)
- 1 banana or 1 cup frozen berries
- 1 tbsp nut butter (optional)
**Swap:** add spinach for micronutrients with no real flavor change
**Est. macros (base):** ~380 kcal · 30P / 40C / 10F
